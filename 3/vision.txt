
	Superphysical Perception

	This Art is taught only by superphysical perception. Exiting the 
	body in full consciousness teaches through observation certain 
	truths regarding states of consciousness and superphysical 
	beings. With practice, it is possible to leave and enter the 
	body at will with ever-increasing freedom, until the experiences 
	out of the body are as real & continuous as those in the body.
	
	Superphysical Beings

	Intelligences or Superphysical Beings are composed of finer 
	material which interpenetrates gross matter and its interspaces, 
	as the human finer bodies are built up.



	The bodies of superphysical beings are composed of the finer 
	materials which interpenetrate gross matter,
