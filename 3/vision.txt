This Art (practice of Philosophy) is taught by spiritual perception. 
	Leaving the physical body in full consciousness teaches through observation 
	truths concerning states of consciousness and superphysical beings.

	Henceforward it is possible to leave & enter the body at will with 
	increasing freedom, until the experiences out of the body are as real & 
	continuous as those in the body.
