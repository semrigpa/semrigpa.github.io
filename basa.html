6. Modifiers

Adjectives (noun modifiers) and adverbs (verb modifiers) have the same form. The adjective is placed before the noun, and the adverb before the verb(?).

6. Verbs

Verbs do not change form. Auxiliary verbs indicate tense.

  • sha ... before indicates the future.

Shanti, Jonatan!

Shanti, Jonatan! (Hello, Jonatan!) is the story of a young Englishman who goes to visit his friend in another European country... or so it would seem.

It is written entirely in Dai Basa.

The story begins with very simple language. The first words in the book are Nang ita shaha (a man stands in a city), and continues from there.




Bahagi Yek (1)

Nang ita shaha. Nang shkua kitab. Nang wa shaha.

Ka nang ita shaha? Si, da ita shaha.
