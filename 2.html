    • Principles: Astral Image
    • Colours: Violet
    • Consciousness: Through instinctual perceptions.
  • 3rd
    • Elements: Air
    • Principles: Kāma
    • Colours: Red
    • Consciousness: Through magnetic perceptions.
  • 4th
    • Elements: Fire
    • Principles: Lower Manas
    • Colours: Green
    • Consciousness: Through psycho-physiological perceptions.
  • 5th
    • Elements: Ether
    • Principles: Higher Manas
    • Colours: Indigo
    • Consciousness: Through purely mental perceptions.
  • 6th
    • Elements: Divine Flame
    • Principles: Buddhi
    • Colours: Yellow
    • Consciousness: Through soul-perceptions.
  • 7th
    • Elements: Akasha
    • Principles: Atmic Aura
    • Colours: Entire prismatic septenary; (when auric, Blue)
    • Consciousness: Spiritual through the auric synthetical perceptions.
