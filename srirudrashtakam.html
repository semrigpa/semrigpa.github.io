About Śrī Rudrāṣṭakam

The Rudrashtakam is a sacred hymn dedicated to Lord Shiva (Rudra). It consists of eight verses that praise the various aspects of Lord Shiva.

This application allows you to explore these powerful Sanskrit verses with their translations and meanings.

Use the dropdown menus to filter by verse number and line to find specific content.

Each card shows:

  • The original Sanskrit text in Devanagari
  • IAST transliteration
  • Word-by-word literal meaning
  • Two different translations (literal and poetic)
  • Links to resources for further study
