Oliso Cito

Edjin cika ya oxnu pula yacai, akto ox takan bano.

Kai Nakbi Ona Cika

Oxnu yat ona tutan anonka oyo.

Pat Yesu anompake:

Isa kiyo aox oyoda bana ixnu yat nuts ona ahoci, Cease not to seek day and night until you find the Mysteries of the Light, which will purify you and make you into refined light and lead you into the Kingdom of Light.

Renounce the whole world and the whole matter therein and all its cares and all its sins, that you may be worthy of the Mysteries of the Light and be saved from all chastisement.
