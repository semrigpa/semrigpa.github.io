Seven Scales of Consciousness
Consciousness is on seven planes. These are the seven scales or shades of consciousness, of the Unit; e.g., in a moment of pleasure or pain; four lower and three higher.
Consciousness
7 Atmic Para-Ego X Spiritual, entirely conscious apperception Because it reaches the Higher Self-conscious Manas.
6 Buddhic Inner-Ego/Buddhi Spiritual-emotional Will perception Volitional perception, the voluntary taking in of an idea; you can regard or disregard physical pain.
5 Manasic Manas-Ego/Manas/Higher Ego/Individual Ego Mental-emotional Mansic discernment of the Lower Manas Manasic self-perception
These are the four lower scales, and belong to the psycho-physiological man.
4 Kama-Manasic Kama-Manas/Lower Manas/Higher Psychic/Personal Ego Passional-emotional Vital perception Physical feeling, sensations of pleasure and pain, of quality.
3 Kama-Pranic Pranic Kama/Psychic/Lower Psychic/Pranic Psychological-emotional Psychic apperception Of astral double, doppelganger, carrying it higher to the [Vital Perception]. Its third plane is psychic: here is the instinct which prevents the kitten going into the water and getting drowned.
2 Astral Astral Instinctual Self-perception or apperception i.e., Self-perception of cell On its second plane things are reversed.
