The Means of Overcoming Samsāra in the Kali-Yug

sarva śruti rahasyaṃ gopyaṃ tat śṛṇu yena kali saṃsāram tariṣyasi | 1 |
bhagavat ādipuruṣasya nārāyaṇa nāmoccāraṇa mātreṇa nirdhūta kalir bhavati | 2 |
iti nārāyaṇasya nāmoccharanaṁ, kāli-kalmaśa-naśanam; nataḥ parataropāyaḥ, sarva-vedeṣu dṛśyate. | 3 | 

  1  Listen to the secret which all Vēdas keep hidden, by which you may overcome Saṃsāra in the Kali-Yug.
  2  Just by reciting the name of Lord Nārāyaṇa, the Original Being, the Kali-Yug is shaken off.
  3  Thus reciting the name of Nārāyaṇa destroys the evil of Kali. For those who seek refuge, no better means is seen in all the Vēdas.
