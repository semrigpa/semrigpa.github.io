Alkemie

Y alkemie est y arte dy transformacion, u lleis elements become intertwined ed formen y nove essencia. (Alchemy is the art of transformation, where elements become intertwined and form a new essence.)

⛧

Hermetisme

Y hermetisme representa y symbiosis dy verites occultas, fusionant les principis dy y macrocosmo ed dy y microcosmo. (Hermeticism represents a symbiosis of occult truths, merging the principles of the macrocosm and the microcosm.)

⛧

Invocation dy sagesse: “Sapientia Hermetica, lux venire ynd terram, illuminare ad infinitum.” (Invocation of wisdom: “Hermetic wisdom, light come to earth, illuminate infinitely.”)
