Afrihili Lesson 1

Introduction

Welcome to Afrihili Lesson 1! In this lesson, we will cover basic greetings and simple sentence structures.

Grammar Notes

Greetings: The most common greetings in Afrihili are:

  • "Habari" - Hello
  • "Nzuri" - Good (response to "Habari")
  • "Jambo" - How are you?

Sentence Structure: In Afrihili, sentences follow a Subject-Verb-Object (SVO) structure similar to English. For example:

  • "Mimi ninasoma vitabu." - I read books.
