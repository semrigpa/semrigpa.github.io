Preliminary Instructions

Concentration

For practical instruction it is first necessary to have acquired the power of concentration. These instructions are of little use to one who has not alreadly attained this power. An order of daily meditaion is necessary. There is no other way of attaining this power of concentration, and without this power, no progress can be made or even begun.

Regular Breathing

By concentration in meditation upon a subject, with the effort of regular breathing (the inhale and exhale occupying the same space of time), the mind may be held so that it is not subject to any other thought.
