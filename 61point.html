61-Point Relaxation (Shavayatra)

61-Point Relaxation is called Shavayatra ("corpse-journey"), as it allows one to drop the shthula sharira (physical body) and move into the sukshma sharira (subtle body).

Energy Points

The exercises focuses on the major energy points in the body. Through stress and negativity, these centres become constricted. This exercise frees the circuitry so that prana (vital energy) can flow freely.

  • Chakras — Ajna, Vishuddha, Anahata, Manipura, & Svadhisthana
  • Marmas — Energy centres where the 3 nadis (energy channels) converge.
