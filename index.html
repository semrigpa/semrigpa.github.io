Kalisantaraṇopaniṣadsār

The Essence of the Kali-Santarana Upanishad

By Josiah A. Lai

A commentary based on a reinterpretation of the original text.

Original Sanskrit Verses
सरव श्रुति रहस्यं गोप्यं तत् शृणु येन कलि संसारं तरिष्यसि |
sarva śruti rahasyaṃ gopyaṃ tat śṛṇu yena kali saṃsāram tariṣyasi |
भगवत् आदिपुरुषस्य नारायणस्य नामोच्चारण मात्रेण निर्दूत कलिर्भवति |
bhagavat ādipuruṣasya nārāyaṇasya nāmoccāraṇa mātreṇa nirdhūta kalir bhavati |
इति षोडश कला वृत्तस्य जीवस्य आवरण विनाशनं ततः प्रकाशते परम ब्रह्म मेघापये रविरश्मि मण्डलीवेति |
iti ṣoḍaśa kalāvṛtasya jīvasya āvaraṇa vināśanam tataḥ prakāśate param brahma meghāpaye raviraśmi maṇdalīveti |
नास्य विधिरिति सर्वदा शुचिरशुचिर्वा पठन् ब्रह्माणः सल्लोक्यं समीप्यं सारूप्यता सायुज्यं एति |
nāsya vidhir iti sarvadā śucir aśucir vā paṭhan brahmāṇaḥ sālōkyaṁ sāmīpyaṁ sārūpyatāṁ sāyujyam eti |
सर्वधर्म परित्याग पापात् सद्यः शुचिताम् आप्नुयात् सद्यो मुच्यते सद्यो मुच्यते इत्युपनिषद् |
sarvadharma parityāga pāpāt sadyaḥ śucitām āpnuyāt sadyo mucyate sadyo mucyate ityupaniṣad |
English Translation

1. Listen to the secret which all Vēdas keep hidden, by which one may overcome Saṃsāra in the Kali-Yug.

2. Just by reciting the name of Lord Nārāyaṇa, the Original Being, the Kali-Yug is shaken off.

3. Thus the sixteen coverings of the soul are dissolved, and the Supreme Reality shines forth, like how the sun's rays appear when the clouds disperse.

4. There is no rule for this, always, whether pure or impure, by reciting the name of God, one attains residence with God, and the same intimacy, form, and oneness with Him.

5. In the abandonment of all religious duties, at once one attains purity from misfortune and vice. Immediately one is liberated; thus says the Upaniṣad.

Commentary

The core (sāra) of the Upaniṣad is clear: reciting the holy names of God, lovingly and joyfully, is the only path to mokṣa (liberation) in this current age of Kali. I sometimes call this 'bhakti japa' (reciting with devotion) to Lord Nārāyaṇa. It is a simple, but profound and effective means of communing with God.
