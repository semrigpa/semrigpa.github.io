    1.1: Singular Articles

    Points to Remember:

    • shyin = (dog, dogs) Nouns have only one form for singular and plural.

    • in shyin = (a dog)

    • ènn ibou = (an owl) If in (a) comes before a vowel, it changes to ènn (an).

    • shyin-la = (the dog)

    Examples

    Yé gin drwat-la palé.

    They have the right to speak.

    Fenm-la shant.

    The woman is singing.

    On gin poul, é on limn yê zèf.

    We have chickens, and we like their eggs.





    1.2: Plurals & Demonstratives

    Points to Remember:

    • shyin-yé = (dogs)

    • shyin-layé = (the dogs)

    • dji tomat = (some tomatoes)

    • sink poul = (five chickens) When counting something, just add the number – no need to use -yé here.

    • nonm-çala = (this/that man)

    • nonm-çayé = (these/those men)

    Examples

    Tou makak trouv sô piti-yé joli.

    Every monkey thinks her children are pretty.

    To volé poul-layé?

    Did you steal the chickens?

    Je mèt dji tomat dan mô gombo.

    I put some tomatoes in my gumbo.

    On gin sink poul é trò koshon.

    We have five chickens and three pigs.





    Lesson 2.1: BANGS

    Points to Remember:

    • In Kréyòl, some adjectives come before the noun, but most come after the noun.

    • There are adjectives that come before nouns. Remember this group of adjectives with the mnemonic: BANGS — beauty, age, newness, goodness, size.

    • Apart from BANGS adjectives, all other adjectives come after the noun they describe.

    Adjective Positions

    bèl wazo
    vyé boug
    nouvo sha
    bon tem
    gran mézon

    beautiful bird
    old man
    new car
    good time
    big house

    But:

    shyin las
    min gosh
    gaçon gaya
    shawi kanay
    moun Kréyòl

    tired dog
    left hand
    healthy biy
    sly racoon
    Creole people





    2.2: Comparatives

    Points to Remember:

    • pli = more ➕

    • pli ... ke = more ... than

    • mwins = less ➖

    • mwins ... ke = less ... than

    • osi ... ke = as ... as 🟰

    More

    Je pli vyé ke twa.
    Mô fiy pli gran ke tô fiy.

    I’m older than you.
    My daughter is taller than your daughter.

    Less

    Vou mwins smat ke mwa.

    You're less smart than me.

    As

    Non, je osi smat ke twa!.

    No, I'm as smart as you.
