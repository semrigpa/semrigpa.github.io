Josiah's Wellness Process

Stage 1

(Perform for 3 days every 10 days)

Ingriedients

  • 8oz grated carrots
  • 1oz anise seeed
  • 2tbsp honey
  • 1oz extract of senna
  • 1.5oz extract of spigelia

Instructions

  1. Boil grated carrots for 30 to 45 minutes, then strain.
  2. Boil the carrot liquid with anise seed for 10 minutes, then strain.
  3. To the liquid, add honey, senna, and spigelia.
  4. Store in a cool dark place.

Take upon an empty stomach, one to two tablespoonfuls of it. Use this prescription for three days out of every ten and keep it up for one month. Then discontinue. Eat no breakfast on the mornings you use this preparation and be sure to avoid animal fats and flesh; use no decayed fruits or vegetables.
