Carrot Medicine 👇

Ingriedients

  • 8oz grated carrots
  • 1oz anise seeed
  • 2tbsp honey
  • 1oz extract of senna
  • 1.5oz extract of spigelia

Instructions

  1. Boil grated carrots for 30 to 45 minutes, then strain.
  2. Boil the carrot liquid with anise seed for 10 minutes, then strain.
  3. To the liquid, add honey, senna, and spigelia.
  4. Store in a cool dark place.

Take for 3 consecutive mornings every 10 days. Keep this up for 1 month, then discontinue. On an empty stomach, take 1 to 2 tablespoons. Eat no breakfast on the mornings you use this preparation.

Avoid animal fats and flesh. Do not use decayed fruit or vegetables.
